 Donating blood is so simple!!


Donation Process:

1) Registration: Provide your details and complete a brief health questionnaire.


2) Screening: A quick check of your vital signs and hemoglobin levels.


3) Donation: The actual blood donation takes about 10-15 minutes.


4) Resfreshment: Enjoy a snack and drink to replenish energy.


5) Rest: Take a short rest before leaving, knowing you've made a significant impact on someone's life.
